The Rules

Timeframe
The scavenger hunt will run from the 3rd of February 7am AEST to the 17th of February 8pm AEST. All tasks must be completed in Japan during this period.

Task Completion
Each task can only be completed once. Duplicate submissions for the same task will not be accepted.

Evidence Submission
To validate a completed task, participants must submit photo or video evidence. If you have any disputes text one of the approvers.

Team Participation
You may participate as an individual or in a team of two. If you are participating as a team, please register under a single name to share your entries. The prize money will be split amongst those in the team.

Account Registration
When signing up for the scavenger hunt, do not use your regular password for security reasons.

Participation Fee
To participate, send $5 to Cam to contribute to the prize pool.

Task Approval
Tasks will be manually reviewed and approved based on the discretion of designated approvers.

Purpose
This scavenger hunt is designed to create lasting memories and collect meaningful photos or videos from your Japan trip.

Respect and Safety
Always be respectful of local customs, laws, and people while completing tasks. Ensure your safety and the safety of others during the hunt.

Prize
There will be 1st, 2nd and 3rd place monetary prizes. Winners will be announced on the 17th of February at 9pm AEST.

Have Fun!
Above all, enjoy the experience, get out of your comfort zone, explore Japan, and make unforgettable memories!
